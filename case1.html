The Story Setting

Gretel Aroma was not in a good mood. The millionaire cosmetics queen and her husband had invited their four most snobbish friends to spend the weekend at their chalet in the Swiss Alps. Shortly after the couples arrived, a blizzard hit, quickly burying the roads in half a meter of snow. That in itself was good. The chalet had plenty of provisions and the snowfall was welcomed. But then, bright and early on Saturday, one of Gretel's guests died of a heart attack. Gretel considered this a great inconvenience.

Paulina Chadwick had hopped on the stationary bicycle, warming up for a day on the slopes. An hour later, when two of the men found her, she was splayed across the handlebars. Hansel Aroma telephoned the nearest village and was told a hearse would come and claim the body. “Tonight, Herr Aroma, or tomorrow, as soon as the road is plowed. Our condolences to you.”
