The Murder Scene

Ten minutes later, Boris led Gretel and the chalet's handyman to the Stuttgart room at the back of the main floor.

"There's this leaky pipe under our sink," he explained as he unlocked the door. The bedroom was cluttered with Belle's amazing array of beauty aids. Glancing between the bald heads of two wig stands, Gretel was the first to see the body. There in a corner she lay, face down in her sexiest robe, her flowing blond hair almost hiding the rope of pantyhose tied around her neck. It seemed that Belle Stuttgart had been murdered.

Gretel knelt down and felt the cold hand. "Oh, Belle!" she cried. "No! No! This ruins the whole weekend."
