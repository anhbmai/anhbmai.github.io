My sculpture is the character Totoro from the movie "My neighbor Totoro". This character represents Ghibli Studio, which also represents my childhood. I feel like a lot of kids grew up watching Ghibli movies such as "Spirited Away", "Ponyo"... and this specific character - Totoro - can be recognized by a lot of people. I loved him as a kid so I decided to build him in Minecraft. This is my first time building in Minecraft so I died 7 times from falling off and getting hit by zombies.
