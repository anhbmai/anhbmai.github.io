When the Police Skied Up Half an Hour Later...

When the police skied up half an hour later, they found Gretel waiting.

"Thank heaven you're here. We didn't tell a soul about this unpleasantness," she whispered as she unlocked the bedroom door.

Wearing gloves, the constable in charge turned the victim's head. "Yes, that's Belle," the hostess said. "Hmm, strangulation does cruel things to one's complexion."

They began with an inspection. "The terrace is unlocked," the second officer said, then looked through the glass door.

"There are lots of footprints outside. And..." He bent over and picked up a note that lay half-hidden under the corpse's left hip.

"My dearest Belle." The note had been typed. "Meet me at the usual spot. I'll be there between 6:00 and 6:30. Be discreet, my darling. The last thing we want is for our mates to find out."

"Very interesting," the strait-laced officer said. And he blushed.
