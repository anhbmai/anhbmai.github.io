Observe People at the Scene

Boris pulled the hostess away, warning her not to touch anything. They left the room, locked the door, then telephoned the police.

"We're sending two officers on skis," the efficient Swiss constable informed her.

Gretel turned from the phone to see Peter Chadwick. He had just come in and was removing a pair of snowshoes.

"Road's still closed," he announced, his nerves frayed almost to the breaking point. "Having my wife just lying around, Hansel, should put pressure on the authorities. Where is Hansel? Is he still on that business call!"

Gretel did her best to calm him, while Boris, the most recent widower, went off for a walk in the woods.
