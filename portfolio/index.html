Intro to Digital Media Art (Art-74)

Hello! My name is Anh Mai. ✨

Welcome to my creative space! This site is my Art 74 portfolio at San José State University. In this class, we focus on the basics of digital media production, including using visualization software and building work for the web. A key foundation of the course is Mark Tribe’s definition of new media art as work that uses emerging technologies to explore their cultural, political, and aesthetic possibilities.

This portfolio shows my experiments with those ideas throughout the course. I hope you enjoy seeing how I blend technology with my own bright and playful style!
