MEME MASHUP

For this assignment I decided to mash up a fresh meme from an anime series called “Danganronpa” with a Chiikawa sticker. The context of the meme is a character from the series – Kirigiri – was found guilty of murdering her classmate in a Battel Royale-ish game and is being punished because of it. Her punishment is being crushed to death and in the meme she is holding her breath until her face turns purple waiting for the gigantic thing behind to crush her.

I incorporated a scared Chiikawa sticker because I love Chiikawa and it fits the context of the meme. Usually people use the meme for situations of waiting anxiously while knowing exactly something bad is about to happen to them.
